Represents the current state of a Trick, with logic to step through the trick, collecting player inputs, and progressing the Trick. NOTE: A single Game is composed of a series of Tricks.

The Caller is expected to keep track of the Players, provide the Players with Cards, and keep track of which player is supposed to begin the Trick.
